FAQ

Common questions before starting a software subscription.

Is PetroSphere a SaaS platform or a service?

PetroSphere is primarily software. Optional onboarding, data migration and training can be added during setup, but the core offer is a subscription product.

How is pricing billed?

Starter and Pro can be billed monthly or annually. Enterprise is typically contracted annually. Onboarding is billed once at the start of implementation.

What data can I load into the platform?

Production history, pressure data, test results, PVT inputs, well metadata and asset hierarchies can be loaded via CSV/XLSX or custom connectors depending on your plan.
