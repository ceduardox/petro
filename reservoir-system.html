Reservoir Forecasting & Reserves System

PetroSphere helps reservoir and asset teams build decline cases, compare forecast assumptions, organize reserve reviews and keep technical outputs in a structured product instead of maintaining fragmented spreadsheets and disconnected review files.

What This System Delivers

Forecast governance without spreadsheet chaos

Better case control Keep assumptions, versions and notes attached to the work.
Clearer reserve review Compare scenarios and export management-ready outputs.
Faster collaboration Share forecast logic across reservoir and production roles.

This system is aimed at teams that need repeatable forecasts, review discipline and more confidence in planning discussions.

Benefits

Why reservoir and asset teams move these workflows into software.

More consistent forecasts

Standardize decline workflows and reduce the variability that comes from personal spreadsheet formats and hidden assumptions.

Versioned reserve cases

Track multiple reserve or planning cases with stored notes, assumptions and comparison views.

Faster technical reviews

Assemble management or partner-facing outputs with less manual formatting and fewer disconnected source files.

Stronger cross-team alignment

Give production, reservoir and planning teams a common place to review the same forecast logic and case history.

3

Export the technical review, summarize the recommendation and keep the case history stored for future planning cycles.
